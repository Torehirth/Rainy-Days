our story

It all started as an idea when me and a buddy of mine were on a skiing trip, both with expensive new jackets. After a week with bad weather and a couple of ski-hiking tours the jackets began to tear up, making them wet and uncomfortable. We couldn't buy new jackets because all of our money was wasted on the trip, and of course on the jackets. Therefor we had to endure the the remaining week beeing constantly wet and miserable!

When we finaly got back home safely from the two week long trip both of us had come up with some brand new ideas for what functionality our jackets should have and what it should endure. keywords as quality, affordable and good looking was our criteria. We sat down and brainstormed a couple of ideas that we were happy with. It didn’t go long before we were in contact with garment suppliers and getting a brand new sewing machine. Back at the house we furnished the basement into our little “factory”.

And this was the start of the brand Rainy Days.
